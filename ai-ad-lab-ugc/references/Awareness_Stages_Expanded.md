AWARENESS STAGES — EXPANDED FOR DTC SHORT-FORM VIDEO
======================================================
How to adjust your ad messaging based on where the customer is in their awareness journey. Every angle, hook, and script must match the awareness stage.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STAGE 1: UNAWARE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Definition: The customer doesn't know they have a problem, or won't admit it. They're not looking for a solution — they're just scrolling.

Psychological job: Make them RECOGNIZE a problem they didn't know they had (or were ignoring). Create a "wait, that's me" moment.

What the ad must do:
- Lead with a relatable SITUATION or IDENTITY, not a product
- Never mention the product in the hook — it's too early
- Create pattern interruption through emotional resonance
- Plant a seed: "Maybe I should look into this"

DTC short-form video approach:
- POV skits showing the problem in a relatable way
- Identity callouts ("If you're a [type of person]...")
- Lifestyle content where the problem is visible but not explicitly named
- Relatable moments everyone experiences but nobody talks about

Hook style: Situational, identity-based, curiosity-driven
Script structure: Relatable situation → "Did you know...?" → Soft introduction → Curiosity CTA
CTA style: Soft — "Look it up" / "Check the link" / "See for yourself"

Best angle types: Question (#7), Call Out Person (#13), POV (#17), Lifestyle Aspiration (#23)

Example hooks:
- "POV: You just realized your skin looks different on camera than in the mirror"
- "Nobody talks about this, but if you're over 30..."
- "Is it just me or does everyone's morning routine take way too long?"

Platform notes:
- TikTok: Unaware content works best as native/organic-feeling POV content
- Meta: Unaware angles need strong visual hooks since the audience isn't searching for solutions
- YouTube Shorts: Educational "did you know" angles work well for unaware

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STAGE 2: PROBLEM AWARE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Definition: The customer KNOWS they have a problem. They feel the pain. But they don't know what type of solution exists, or they've tried things that didn't work.

Psychological job: VALIDATE the pain ("you're not alone, this is real"), INTENSIFY it ("and it's worse than you think"), then POINT toward a solution category.

What the ad must do:
- Lead with the PROBLEM — call it out directly using their language (VOC)
- Make the customer feel understood ("this person gets me")
- Agitate the pain: show the cost of NOT solving it (wasted money, wasted time, embarrassment, missed opportunities)
- Introduce the solution CATEGORY (not the product yet — that's Solution Aware's job)

DTC short-form video approach:
- "If you're dealing with [pain], watch this" — direct callout hooks
- "The real reason [problem] keeps happening" — root cause education
- "I tried everything for [problem] until..." — failed alternative stories
- Empathy-first scripts that validate before solving

Hook style: Pain-calling, empathy-led, "I've been there", diagnostic
Script structure: Call out pain → Validate/agitate → Reveal root cause → Introduce solution path → Information CTA
CTA style: Information — "Link in bio to learn more" / "I'll put the link below"

Best angle types: Speed (#2), Remove Limitations (#6), Challenge Beliefs (#11), Call Out Solution (#12), Call Out Person (#13), Problem-Aware (#14), POV (#17), If-Then (#18), Creator Authority (#22)

Example hooks:
- "If your skin looks dull no matter what you do, it's probably not your products"
- "Stop scrolling if you've tried everything for [problem]"
- "The mistake 90% of people make with [category]"

Platform notes:
- TikTok: Direct pain callouts with fast pacing. "If you're..." hooks are native to TikTok.
- Meta: Problem-aware ads perform well in Feed — viewers have time to engage with the pain
- YouTube Shorts: "Here's why" and root-cause angles work well

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STAGE 3: SOLUTION AWARE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Definition: The customer knows solutions exist. They may have tried competitors. They're actively comparing options but don't know YOUR product yet.

Psychological job: DIFFERENTIATE your product from the alternatives. Show why your solution is the #1 best option in the category. Overcome "they're all the same" thinking.

What the ad must do:
- Lead with the SOLUTION CATEGORY, then position your product as the best one
- Address the "I've tried things like this before" objection head-on
- Show what makes your product DIFFERENT (ingredient, mechanism, approach, results)
- Prove it with specific evidence (not generic claims)

DTC short-form video approach:
- "I've tried 10 [product types] — this is the only one that..." — comparison/authority
- "What nobody tells you about [product category]" — insider knowledge
- "The difference between [your product] and everything else is..." — mechanism spotlight
- Side-by-side comparisons with competitors (without naming them negatively)

Hook style: Comparison, "here's what's different", myth-busting, insider knowledge
Script structure: Acknowledge category → Differentiate → Show mechanism → Prove with results → Direct CTA
CTA style: Comparison — "See why people are switching" / "Try it and compare"

Best angle types: Size (#1), Compare (#5), Remove Limitations (#6), Information (#8), Newness (#9), Exclusiveness (#10), Challenge Beliefs (#11), Features to Benefits (#15), Routine Integration (#19), Social Proof Stack (#21), Ingredient Spotlight (#24)

Example hooks:
- "Every [product type] I've tried did [common failure] — until this one"
- "Here's what most [product category] brands won't tell you"
- "I switched from [competitor type] and here's why I'm not going back"

Platform notes:
- TikTok: Comparison and "here's the difference" content is highly shareable
- Meta: Solution-aware ads work across Feed and Reels — viewers are in evaluation mode
- YouTube Shorts: Educational comparison content performs well

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STAGE 4: PRODUCT AWARE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Definition: The customer knows YOUR product exists. They've seen it, maybe visited the site, maybe even added to cart. But they haven't bought yet — something is holding them back.

Psychological job: REMOVE the last objections. Provide the proof, details, and reassurance they need to commit.

What the ad must do:
- Lead with the PRODUCT NAME — they already know it
- Address specific objections (price, "does it really work?", "is it right for ME?")
- Stack proof: reviews, before/afters, expert endorsements, specific results
- Make buying feel safe (guarantees, social proof, risk reversal)

DTC short-form video approach:
- Honest reviews with specific timelines and results
- Unboxing + first impression + follow-up results
- FAQ-style objection handling ("Is it worth $X?")
- Customer compilation / social proof stacking

Hook style: Review, proof, objection handling, "X months later"
Script structure: Product name → Handle #1 objection → Proof stack → Specific benefit/result → Direct CTA
CTA style: Direct — "Grab yours at [brand].com" / "Link in bio"

Best angle types: Size (#1), Speed (#2), Authority (#3), Before/After (#4), Compare (#5), Features to Benefits (#15), Review (#16), Unboxing (#20), Social Proof Stack (#21), Ingredient Spotlight (#24)

Example hooks:
- "Honest review of [product] after 90 days"
- "Is [product] actually worth $[price]? Let's find out"
- "I was skeptical about [product] but look at my results"

Platform notes:
- TikTok: Honest, unfiltered review content. "Is it worth it?" format is native.
- Meta: Product-aware ads work well as retargeting (people who visited your site)
- YouTube Shorts: Longer review content with more detail

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STAGE 5: MOST AWARE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Definition: The customer knows your product, knows what it does, and WANTS it. They just need a reason to buy NOW.

Psychological job: Create URGENCY. Give them the final push with an offer, deadline, or compelling reason to stop procrastinating.

What the ad must do:
- Lead with the OFFER or DEAL — they don't need education
- Create urgency (limited time, limited stock, price increase)
- Reduce risk (guarantee, free shipping, easy returns)
- Make the action clear and immediate

DTC short-form video approach:
- Sale announcement with countdown
- "Last chance" urgency content
- Bundle/value stacking ("here's everything you get")
- Restock announcement ("it's finally back")
- Loyalty/repurchase ("I'm on my 5th bottle — here's why")

Hook style: Offer-first, urgency, "last chance", value stacking
Script structure: Offer → Why now → Quick reminder of value → Urgency CTA
CTA style: Urgency — "Use code [X] before [deadline]" / "[X]% off ends [date]" / "Link in bio — limited stock"

Best angle types: Size (#1), Authority (#3), Exclusiveness (#10), Review (#16), Unboxing (#20), Social Proof Stack (#21)

Example hooks:
- "[Product] is [X]% off right now — this never happens"
- "If you've been thinking about trying [product], this is the time"
- "They finally restocked and I'm grabbing 3"

Platform notes:
- TikTok: Flash sale and urgency content. Keep it fast and direct.
- Meta: Most-aware ads are ideal for retargeting with offer overlays
- YouTube Shorts: Value stacking ("everything you get for $X") works well
